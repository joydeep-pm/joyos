# Executive Status Snapshot

## Snapshot Details
- Topic: FY27 Product Strategy and Roadmap Status
- Audience: Leadership / business stakeholders
- Date: 2026-03-21

## One-Line Read

FY27 strategy is directionally clear, but current success depends on protecting a small number of parity and compliance bets strongly enough that roadmap progress becomes commercially credible.

## Headline Metrics / Facts

- FY26 confirmed India losses: ₹100.5 Cr
- Demo loss rate: 78.5%
- Major visible parity gaps remain in Gold, LAS / LAMF, and BNPL / Credit Line
- FY27 roadmap scale is roughly 5,385–5,551 PD with execution concentration risk

## What Matters Most Right Now

1. Protect the highest-value parity and compliance bets from dilution.
2. Keep compliance positioned as a business gate, not technical polish.
3. Improve business confidence through referenceability, specialist clarity, and stronger communication packaging.

## Current Risks

- adjacent complexity weakens focus on protected bets
- compliance follow-through remains vulnerable to under-ownership
- new-market narratives can outrun actual readiness and specialist support

## Leadership Asks

- confirm the 3-5 non-negotiable roadmap bets for the current period
- force explicit ownership on specialist dependencies behind high-upside bets

## Near-Term Outlook

The next strong signal of progress will be visible quarter protection, a recurring win/loss review rhythm, and clearer ownership of specialist dependencies behind roadmap promises.
