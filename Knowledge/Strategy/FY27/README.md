# FY27 Strategy Knowledge Area

This folder is the durable Personal OS source of truth for the FY27 product strategy corpus.

It replaces the role of the browser-local command-center HTML as the primary strategy layer.

## Purpose

Use this area to maintain:
- executive strategy framing
- FY26 lessons learned
- win/loss analysis
- AOP and roadmap framing
- LOS / AI-first expansion strategy
- source traceability back to the original documents

## Canonical Files

- `Executive-Snapshot.md` — top-level FY27 business and strategy summary
- `Strategic-Imperatives.md` — the major strategic bets and why they matter
- `FY26-Lessons-Learned.md` — key lessons from losses, demos, and execution patterns
- `Win-Loss-Analysis.md` — root-cause view of deal losses and recovery paths
- `AOP-vs-Roadmap.md` — planning, capacity, and sequencing summary
- `LOS-Strategy.md` — LOS / AI-first origination and data-master strategy
- `Operating-Command-Brief.md` — the fastest leadership-ready read
- `Source-Index.md` — traceability to HTML, PDFs, and workbook sheets

## Operating Rule

These markdown notes are the durable strategy layer.
The standalone HTML command center is a source/reference artifact, not the system of record.
