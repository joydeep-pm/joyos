# FY27 Current Shareable Status

**Audience:** Business stakeholders / leadership / roadmap conversations  
**Updated:** 2026-03-21

## Executive Summary

Finflux’s FY27 strategy is centered on converting known market opportunity into wins by closing a small number of high-impact parity gaps, removing compliance vetoes, and protecting delivery quality strongly enough that roadmap progress becomes commercially believable.

At the moment, the most important leadership priority is not inventing new strategy. It is protecting the few bets that matter most and making sure they are not diluted by adjacent scope, under-owned dependencies, or weak referenceability.

## Current Business Read

### What is working in the strategy
- The business has a clear view of the major revenue leakage areas.
- The roadmap has identifiable recovery logic tied to key gaps.
- Strategic themes such as compliance, new-market entry, and AI leverage are now clearly framed.

### What needs active attention now
- protecting the most commercially important parity bets
- treating compliance as a business gate, not technical polish
- preventing complexity expansion from crowding out higher-certainty work
- improving referenceability and commercial confidence alongside product progress

## Current Protected Priorities

### Priority 1: High-value parity recovery
Primary areas:
- Gold
- LAS / LAMF
- BNPL / Credit Line baseline parity

### Priority 2: Compliance firewall
Primary areas:
- DPDPA
- ECL / provisioning maturity
- EIR / accounting rigor
- audit / governance depth

### Priority 3: Stability and referenceability
Primary areas:
- stabilization / bug-bash discipline
- implementation confidence
- proof and collateral readiness

## Current Watchouts

- co-lending complexity may expand faster than justified relative to clearer recovery bets
- compliance follow-through may remain under-owned if not explicitly protected
- new-market narratives can outrun actual specialist readiness
- product progress may not convert commercially if trust/referenceability remains weak

## Current Leadership Actions Underway

- quarter-protection review created to separate protected bets from deferrable work
- win/loss review template created to support recurring commercial learning
- specialist dependency matrix created to expose capability gaps behind new-market bets

## Near-Term Outlook

Over the next few weeks, the strongest signal of progress will be clearer protection of the most commercially important roadmap items, a more explicit win/loss operating loop, and visible ownership of specialist dependencies behind high-upside bets.
