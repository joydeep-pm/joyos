# Workflows

Reusable workflows that the assistant invokes on demand. These are referenced from `AGENTS.md` and should support Joydeep’s Director-of-Products operating model rather than generic productivity.

## Daily Workflows

| Workflow | Time | When to Use |
|----------|------|-------------|
| [Morning Standup](morning-standup.md) | 5 min | Start of day — produce a Director Intervention Brief and Today’s Three |
| [Backlog Processing](backlog-processing.md) | 5-10 min | Convert raw capture into structured tasks tied to goals and context |
| [Meeting Follow-Up](meeting-followup.md) | 10 min | Turn meeting notes into tasks, follow-ups, and knowledge updates |

## Weekly Workflows

| Workflow | Time | When to Use |
|----------|------|-------------|
| [Weekly Review](weekly-review.md) | 20-30 min | Review what moved, what stalled, what repeated, and what matters next |
| [1:1 Prep](one-on-one-prep.md) | 10-15 min | Prepare PM coaching conversations and support follow-through |

## Skill Workflows

| Workflow | Triggers On | Description |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| [Content Generation](content-generation.md) | writing, content, outreach, marketing tasks | Generate content in the user’s authentic voice |

## Quick Reference: Common Prompts

**Morning**
- "What needs my intervention today?"
- "What are my Today's Three?"
- "What's the one thing I should definitely unblock today?"

**During the day**
- "Process my backlog"
- "Turn this meeting note into follow-ups"
- "I'm blocked on [task] because [reason]"
- "Prep my 1:1 with [PM name]"

**Planning**
- "What is stale across my PM team?"
- "Which feature requests need attention?"
- "Help me plan next week"

**Reflection**
- "What moved Documentation, Stability, or New Business this week?"
- "Where did I have to intervene this week?"
- "What should this Personal OS learn from this week?"
